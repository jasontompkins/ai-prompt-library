# Art Styles for Midjourney Prompts

1. **Watercolor** — Soft, translucent washes of color with fluid, dreamy edges
2. **Oil Painting** — Rich, textured brushwork with deep, vibrant colors
3. **Impressionism** — Loose brushstrokes capturing light and movement
4. **Surrealism** — Dreamlike, bizarre imagery blending reality and fantasy
5. **Art Nouveau** — Elegant, flowing lines inspired by natural forms
6. **Cyberpunk** — Neon-lit, futuristic dystopian aesthetic
7. **Steampunk** — Victorian-era meets industrial machinery and brass gears
8. **Anime** — Japanese animation style with expressive characters
9. **Pixel Art** — Retro, blocky digital art reminiscent of early video games
10. **Minimalism** — Clean lines, limited color palettes, and simple shapes
11. **Bauhaus** — Geometric shapes, primary colors, and functional design
12. **Pop Art** — Bold colors, comic-style imagery inspired by mass culture
13. **Ukiyo-e** — Traditional Japanese woodblock print style
14. **Gothic** — Dark, dramatic, and ornate medieval-inspired aesthetic
15. **Vaporwave** — Retro 80s/90s aesthetic with pastel purples and pinks
16. **Low Poly** — 3D geometric shapes with flat, angular surfaces
17. **Sketch/Pencil Drawing** — Raw, hand-drawn linework with shading
18. **Baroque** — Dramatic lighting, rich detail, and grandeur
19. **Street Art/Graffiti** — Urban, bold, spray-painted aesthetic
20. **Art Deco** — Glamorous, geometric, and symmetrical 1920s style
21. **Pointillism** — Images composed entirely of small dots of color
22. **Concept Art** — Detailed, illustrative style used in games and films
23. **Flat Design** — Simple, two-dimensional shapes with solid colors
24. **Chiaroscuro** — Strong contrast between light and dark for dramatic effect
25. **Glitch Art** — Digital distortion and corrupted data aesthetics
